The artist, Jonesy, started drawing on the walls of the rented apartment with permanent markers at the age of 2. Since then, she has dedicated her free time filling sketch pads, canvases and other medium(yes, including walls) with fun and original creations.

All this is aimed at living a life immersed in art and creation. In a few more years, she hopes to use all she has learned doing what she loves for a living.
